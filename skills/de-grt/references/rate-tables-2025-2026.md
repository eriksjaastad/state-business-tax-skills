# Delaware Gross Receipts Tax Rate Tables — 2025-2026

## Major Business Categories

Rates and monthly exclusions for the most common business categories. For the complete 50+ category schedule, see the [official rate PDF](https://revenuefiles.delaware.gov/docs/gr_rates.pdf).

| Category | Rate | Monthly Exclusion | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Retailers | 0.7468% | $100,000 | [DE Div of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| Wholesalers | 0.3983% | $10,000 | [DE Div of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| Manufacturers | 0.0945% | $1,250,000 | [DE Div of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| General services / occupations | 0.3983% | $100,000 | [DE Div of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| Contractors | 0.6534% | $100,000 | [DE Div of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| Food processors | 0.1883% | $100,000 | [DE Div of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| Petroleum products | Variable (up to 2.4218%) | Varies | [DE Div of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |

Overall rate range: 0.0945% – 1.9914% (petroleum up to 2.4218%).

> Complete rate schedule: [Delaware Division of Revenue Rates PDF](https://revenuefiles.delaware.gov/docs/gr_rates.pdf)

## Filing Thresholds

| Frequency | Criteria | Due Date | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly | Higher-revenue businesses | 20th of following month | [DE GRT FAQs](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| Quarterly | Smaller / new businesses | Last day of month after quarter | [DE GRT FAQs](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |

Filing frequency assigned based on look-back period. New businesses default to quarterly. All filing is electronic via [tax.delaware.gov](https://tax.delaware.gov).

## Penalties

| Penalty | Amount | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Late filing | 5% per month | [DE GRT FAQs](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| Late interest | 0.5% per month | [DE GRT FAQs](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
| Non-payment | 1% monthly (max 25%) | [DE GRT FAQs](https://revenue.delaware.gov/frequently-asked-questions/gross-receipts-tax-faqs/) |
