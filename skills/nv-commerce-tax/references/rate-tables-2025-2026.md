## Filing Thresholds

| Item | Detail | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue threshold | $4,000,000 NV gross revenue | [NRS 363C](https://www.leg.state.nv.us/nrs/NRS-363C.html) |
| Fiscal year | July 1 – June 30 | [NRS 363C](https://www.leg.state.nv.us/nrs/NRS-363C.html) |
| Due date | August 14 (45 days after June 30) | [NV Dept of Taxation](https://tax.nv.gov/tax-types/commerce-tax/) |
| Extension | Up to 30 days; interest at 0.75%/month | [NRS 363C](https://www.leg.state.nv.us/nrs/NRS-363C.html) |
