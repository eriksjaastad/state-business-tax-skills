# Ohio CAT Rate Tables — 2025-2026

## Tax Rate (ORC Chapter 5751)

| Component | Amount | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Tax rate | 0.26% (0.0026) | [Brady Ware — CAT Update](https://bradyware.com/ohio-commercial-activity-tax-cat-update/) |
| Exclusion threshold (2025+) | $6,000,000 | [Brady Ware — CAT Update](https://bradyware.com/ohio-commercial-activity-tax-cat-update/) |
| Annual Minimum Tax | Eliminated (2024) | [RubinBrown — CAT Changes](https://www.rubinbrown.com/insights-events/insight-articles/2025-changes-to-ohio-commercial-activity/) |

Single flat rate. No tiers, no classifications, no cost subtractions.

> Legislation: [ORC Chapter 5751](https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/chapter-5751)

## Filing Thresholds

| Threshold | Amount | Obligation | Source |
|---|---|---|---|

## Nexus Thresholds (Bright-Line)

| Nexus Trigger | Threshold | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Ohio taxable gross receipts | $500,000 | [ORC 5751.01](https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-5751.01) |
| Ohio property | $50,000 | [ORC 5751.01](https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-5751.01) |
| Ohio payroll | $50,000 | [ORC 5751.01](https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-5751.01) |
| 25% of total property, payroll, or receipts | Any amount | [ORC 5751.01](https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-5751.01) |

## Ownership Aggregation

Individuals with >50% ownership in multiple businesses must combine gross receipts across entities to determine if the $6M threshold is met.

> Source: [Brady Ware — CAT Update](https://bradyware.com/ohio-commercial-activity-tax-cat-update/)
