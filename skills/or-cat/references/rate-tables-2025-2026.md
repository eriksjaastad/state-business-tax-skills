# Oregon CAT Rate Tables — 2025-2026

## Tax Rate (ORS 317A, effective January 1, 2020)

| Component | Amount | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Base tax | $250 | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Rate on taxable commercial activity over $1M | 0.57% (0.0057) | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |

No tiered rates. No classification-based rates. All business types pay the same rate.

> Legislation: [ORS Chapter 317A](https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors317A.html)

## 35% Cost Subtraction

| Subtraction Option | Calculation | Cap | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cost inputs | 35% × COGS (federal method) | 95% of commercial activity | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Labor costs | 35% × (wages + health insurance + retirement) | 95% of commercial activity | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |

Use the **greater** of cost inputs or labor costs. Per-employee labor cost cap: $500,000 (excludes employer payroll taxes).

> Instructions: [2025 Form OR-CAT Instructions](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/forms/FormsPubs/form-or-cat-instr_106-003-1_2025.pdf)

### Restrictions

- No deduction for transactions between unitary group members
- No deduction for costs associated with excluded commercial activity receipts

## Registration and Filing Thresholds

| Threshold | Amount | Obligation | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| No obligations | ≤$750,000 | None | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Registration required | >$750,000 | Register within 30 days | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Filing + payment required | >$1,000,000 | File annual return + pay tax | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Estimated payments required | ≥$5,000 expected liability | Quarterly estimated payments | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |

## Penalties

| Penalty Type | Amount | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Failure to register | $100/month (max $1,000/year) | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Quarterly underpayment (<90% paid) | 5% | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Late filing (≤3 months) | 5% of tax due | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Late filing (>3 months) | 20% of tax due | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Non-payment after demand | 25% of tax due (within 30 days of notice) | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |
| Three-year non-filing | 100% of tax due | [Oregon DOR CAT](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/corporate-activity-tax.aspx) |

Maximum total penalties cannot exceed 100% of tax due.
