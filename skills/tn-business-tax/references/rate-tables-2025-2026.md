# Tennessee Business Tax Rate Tables — 2025-2026

|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1A | Food/beer for home consumption | 0.100% | 0.025% | [TN DOR Rates](https://www.tn.gov/revenue/taxes/business-tax/due-dates-and-tax-rates.html) |

| 1D | Gasoline/diesel fuel (retail only) | 0.050% | — | [TN DOR Rates](https://www.tn.gov/revenue/taxes/business-tax/due-dates-and-tax-rates.html) |

| 2 | General merchandise (clothing, vehicles, furniture, drugs, restaurants) | 0.150% | 0.0375% | [TN DOR Rates](https://www.tn.gov/revenue/taxes/business-tax/due-dates-and-tax-rates.html) |
| 3 | Services + specialty retail (antiques, books, jewelry, sporting goods) | 0.1875% | 0.0375% | [TN DOR Rates](https://www.tn.gov/revenue/taxes/business-tax/due-dates-and-tax-rates.html) |

## Filing Thresholds

| Threshold | Amount | Obligation | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Registration threshold | $100,000 per jurisdiction | Must register and file | [TN DOR — Business Tax](https://www.tn.gov/revenue/taxes/business-tax.html) |
| Deemed location threshold | $50,000 contracts in another jurisdiction | Must file for that jurisdiction | [TCA § 67-4-709](https://law.justia.com/codes/tennessee/title-67/chapter-4/part-7/section-67-4-709/) |

## Filing Due Dates

| Filing Type | Due Date | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Annual return | April 15 (15th of 4th month after fiscal year end) | [TN DOR Rates](https://www.tn.gov/revenue/taxes/business-tax/due-dates-and-tax-rates.html) |
| Final return (closure) | Within 15 days of closure | [TN DOR — Business Tax](https://www.tn.gov/revenue/taxes/business-tax.html) |
