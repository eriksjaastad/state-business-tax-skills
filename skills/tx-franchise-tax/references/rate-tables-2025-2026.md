## Margin Calculation Methods

Taxable margin = total revenue minus the **lowest** of:

| Method | Formula | Source |
|---|---|---|
| 70% of revenue | Total revenue × 0.70 | [TX Comptroller Overview](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/publications/98-806.php) |
| Revenue minus COGS | Total revenue − cost of goods sold | [TX Comptroller Overview](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/publications/98-806.php) |
| Revenue minus compensation | Total revenue − compensation (subject to cap) | [TX Comptroller Overview](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/publications/98-806.php) |
| Revenue minus $1M | Total revenue − $1,000,000 | [TX Comptroller Overview](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/publications/98-806.php) |

> Statute: [Texas Tax Code Chapter 171](https://statutes.capitol.texas.gov/Docs/TX/htm/TX.171.htm)

## Per-Person Compensation Cap

| Report Year | Cap | Source |
|---|---|---|
| 2026 | $480,000 | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |
| 2025 | $450,000 | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |
| 2024 | $430,000 | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |

Includes W-2 wages and officer/owner compensation. Excludes 1099 contractor payments. Adjusted annually for inflation.

## EZ Computation

| Item | Detail | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Eligibility | Total revenue ≤$20,000,000 | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |
| Rate | 0.331% of total revenue | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |
| Restrictions | Cannot use COGS, compensation, or $1M deduction; cannot claim credits | [TX Comptroller Overview](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/publications/98-806.php) |

## Filing

| Item | Detail | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Due date | May 15 | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |
| Late penalty (1-30 days) | 5% of tax + $50 | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |
| Late penalty (>30 days) | 10% of tax + $50 | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |
| Interest | Accrues 61+ days after due date | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/) |
| Filing method | Webfile at comptroller.texas.gov | [TX Comptroller](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/file-pay/) |
