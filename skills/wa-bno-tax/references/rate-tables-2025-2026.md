## Small Business Credit — Service & Other Activities

### Monthly Credit: $160.00
### Quarterly Credit: $480.00
### Annual Credit: $1,920.00

Eligibility: Must report ≥50% of total B&O taxable amount under Service & Other Activities, Real Estate Brokers, or Contests of Chance.

Non-service businesses: $55/month ($165/quarter, $660/year).

### Phase-Out Schedule (Quarterly)

| Quarterly Gross Receipts | Credit Amount | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| $0 – $28,000 | $480.00 (full) | Fixed |
| $28,001 – $55,999 | $480 × (1 - (gross - 28000) / 28000) | Linear decline |
| $56,000+ | $0.00 | No credit |

### Phase-Out Schedule (Annual)

| Annual Gross Receipts | Credit Amount | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| $0 – $112,000 | $1,920.00 (full) | Fixed |
| $112,001 – $223,999 | $1,920 × (1 - (gross - 112000) / 112000) | Linear decline |
| $224,000+ | $0.00 | No credit |

## High-Grossing Business Surcharge (2026-2029)

- Applies to businesses with ≥$250M in WA taxable income
- Rate: 0.5% on income exceeding $250M
- Exemptions: manufacturing, food/prescription drug sales, timber, petroleum
- Not applicable to small businesses

## Future Changes

### Effective January 1, 2027
- Standard rate for manufacturing, extracting, wholesaling increases to 0.5% (from ~0.484%)

### Effective January 1, 2029
- Small business credit doubles: Service $160→$320/month, Non-service $55→$110/month
- Effectively exempts first $250K of income for non-service businesses
